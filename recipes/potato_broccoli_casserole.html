Potato Broccoli Casserole

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious casserole with potatos and broccoli.

Ingredients

  • 800 g potatoes
  • 500 g broccoli
  • three tablespoons flour
  • three tablespoons vegan butter
  • 500 ml plant milk
  • some vegan cheese
  • italian herbs and salt
    •

    Steps

    1. Make a bechamel sauce
    2. Peel potatoes
    3. Cut potatoes and broccoli
    4. Mix everything together and top it with cheese
    5. Bake for 30 minutes and 200 degrees celsius in the oven
