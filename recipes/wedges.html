Potato Wedges with creamy dip

Description

This is a recipe for crispy potato wedges.

Ingredients

  • one kg potatoes
  • vegan creme fraiche
  • oil
  • your favorite herbs
    •

    Steps

    1. Cut potatoes
    2. Put oil and herbs on the potatoes
    3. Bake for 30 minutes and 200 degrees celsius in the oven
    4. Mix the creme fraiche with some herbs
