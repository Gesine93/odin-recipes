Vegan Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious and easy to make vegan lasagna. The amout of ingredients should be sufficient for at least two people.

Ingredients

  • 200 g lasagna sheets
  • 500 g tomato sauce
  • one can of champignons
  • 100 g soy granules
  • three tablespoons flour
  • three tablespoons vegan butter
  • 500 ml plant milk
  • some vegan cheese
  • italian herbs and salt

Steps

  1. Heat the tomato sauce and add the herbs, the soy granules and the champignons
  2. Make a bechamel sauce
  3. Layer the sheets and sauces and put cheese on top
  4. Bake for 30 minutes and 200 degrees celsius in the oven
